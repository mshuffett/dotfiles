## 🗂 Before reorganizing / parking his systems — CHECK THIS FIRST
When about to **reorganize, move, archive, bulk-edit, file, park, or triage** his Todoist / Notion / notes / plans (or propose a workflow change) — we're in an active **calibration phase**, so:
- **Item-level sign-off, not bucket counts** — name each item, its destination, and its review time; he can't approve "~8 items." Act autonomously only where clearly non-destructive/approved. Don't make him re-declare the trust level → auto-memory `system-change-visibility` + coach memory.
- **Park-with-a-review-time** — every parked/moved item gets a concrete date (resurfaces in Todoist) OR a home explicitly walked in the Saturday weekly review; no unreviewed backlogs → auto-memory `park-with-review-time` + coach memory (extends the Due-Date Rule).
