# Email Preferences

Personal preferences for email drafting. Updated by `--learn-style` and manual edits.

## Scheduling

- **Calendar link**: https://cal.com/everythingai/15min and https://cal.com/everythingai/30min
- **Default meeting duration**: 30 min
- **Timezone**: America/Chicago

## Response Style

- **Signature**: Uses 'Best,' 'Cheers,' 'See you there!' or just their first name 'Michael'. Often includes a small icon at the end.
- **Tone**: Professional but warm and casual. Friendly and approachable while maintaining efficiency. Uses exclamation points to convey enthusiasm.
- **Length preference**: Concise (2-3 sentences when possible)

## Templates

### Scheduling Request
```text

```

### Decline Meeting
```text
Thanks for thinking of me, but I'll have to pass on this one.
```

### Need More Context
```text
Thanks for reaching out! Could you share a bit more context on [X]?
```

### Acknowledge and Will Follow Up
```text
Got it, thanks! I'll take a look and get back to you [TIMEFRAME].
```
