### 2. Full Pitch Script (2 minutes)

Once we have your vertebrae, we'll build your full pitch script. The structure from the YC guide:

2. **Two-sentence description** -- already locked
3. **Traction / progress** -- what you've accomplished and how fast (include when you launched and growth rate if you have it)

You have 2 minutes, not the standard YC 60 seconds. More room, but the same principle: don't fill extra time with filler. Short sentences, plain language, speak slowly.
