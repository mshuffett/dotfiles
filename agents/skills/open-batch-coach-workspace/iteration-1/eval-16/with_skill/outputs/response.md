**Demo Day** (March 10, 4:00 PM PT) is the main event. You get 2 minutes in front of 100+ investors at Intercom HQ (or virtually). The goal isn't to close a deal on stage -- it's to clearly explain what you do, get investors excited, and set up follow-up meetings where the real fundraising conversations happen.

Same pitch, very different audiences and stakes. Practice sessions are for you to get better. Demo Day is for investors to decide if they want to talk to you.
