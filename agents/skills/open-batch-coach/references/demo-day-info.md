## Format

- **Audience:** 100+ investors RSVP'd
- **Time per founder:** 2 minutes maximum
- **Slides:** 1-2 slides allowed (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Demo video:** Can include a demo video on the second slide, uploaded to YouTube. Total presentation (including video) must still be 2 minutes max.

## Purpose

The purpose of Demo Day is:

1. Clearly introduce your company to a large number of investors
2. Get investors excited about your company and your progress
3. Arrange follow-up meetings with investors where you can present fully and attempt to raise money
